Here's my story:

Hi! I'm Beatriz and I'm a Process Development Engineer at Embraer. First I worked as support and systems administrator at UNISUL. Later, I joined the Integrar Ulife team as a QA analyst and moved to software development at Anima Educação, which took over UNISUL in 2021. I started my love story with Python when used to automate some processes in the university's infrastructure area. My first project was using Selenium to develop a bot to automate the registration of telephone passwords in Unisul's OSV for more than two thousand employees. I love technology and I hope I can help people with it!
