Fuzzy System:

Development of a Shell for the Automatic Generation Fuzzy Systems.

About Fuzzy

This article presents the development of a Shell for the automatic generation of Fuzzy systems, which allows the definition of linguistic variables, the degrees of relevance of these variables, and the standards that will be used in the process of Fuzzification and Defuzzification. The tests were carried out with a group of students from the Computer Science course at Unisul, which were aimed to compare the efficiency of the developed Shell and an already consolidated framework. The results achieved in this process ensured the effectiveness of the program developed in the treatment of inaccurate data and help for decision support in fuzzy systems.
